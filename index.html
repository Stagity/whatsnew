General FAQ

1. How do I know my destination is safe and open for travel?

Your safety is our priority. Memories Travel always pay close attention to current conditions, from consulting with all applicable governmental agencies to regional stringent rules and regulations. We will inform you should the conditions changes and if there is a possibility that we may not operate the trip.

2. What happens if a destination is deemed unsafe before or during travel?

We will not operate any of our packages unless we are confident that we can keep you safe and healthy and provide a high-quality Memories Travel experience. This has been our philosophy since Day 1. In case of travel restrictions or being unable to operate, we will work with you to modify your travel plans with no fees or penalties.

3. What if I want to reschedule or cancel my trip?

Our packages are offered till end of December 2021 which means you may choose to travel during a time that fits your travel schedule. Changes or cancellation after your final payment due date are subject to fees, which may vary by package. For more information and inquires please contact our sales team at +95 (9) 534-2939, +95 (9) 25405-2298 or email at whatsnew@memories-travel.com.

4. Are face masks required on tour? Will you be providing masks & hand sanitizer?

Yes. For everyone’s safety, we require face masks to be worn while touring public places, particularly indoors, and on public transport included in certain package itinerary. We recommend for you to wear a face mask whenever possible. You may also bring your own face mask and hand sanitizer. We have extras on hand, but supplies may be limited.

5. What if I become ill before I depart or while travelling?

If you are not feeling well or are diagnosed with mild symptoms of sickness before you depart, you should not travel, and you encourage to rebook your trip. Please contact us as soon as possible, and we will do our best to help you reschedule your trip. Changes or cancellation after your final payment due date are subject to fees, which may vary by package.

6. Do I have to pay up front and what forms of payment do you accept?

Yes. All programs can be purchased via online with MPU, Visa, Mastercard, AMEX, 123 Service, and other Wallets. However, we may place a hold on your booking up to three days if you wish to pay in cash in person or via bank transfer. Please contact our sales team at +95 (9) 534-2939, +95 (9) 25405-2298 or email at whatsnew@memories-travel.com should you wish to make a cash payment at Memories Travel office in Pun Hlaing.

7. Can I bring my children?

We do have some age restrictions for certain packages. Download the package itinerary for free since some packages have different requirements.
